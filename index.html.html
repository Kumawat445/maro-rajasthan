Mount Abu, a short distance form jodhur and udaipur, is home to many religious monuments, the most famous being the Dilwara temple, Bramhakumari Ashram, Guru Shikhar and the shrines of jainism. It is a peacefull getway as well as an ideal place for sacred pilgrimage.
